Grilled Tilapia

Yummy Dinner

A recipe for grilled tilapia for a delicious dinner

Ingredients

  • Tilapia
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cover Tilipia in olive oil
  2. Season it with salt, pepper, and paprika and let it marinate for 30 minutes
  3. Put skilet with olive oil on fire over medium-high heat
  4. Fry tilapia on each side for 4-5 minutes, depedning on thickness of fish
