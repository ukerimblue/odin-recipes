Toast with Avocado and Eggs

Breakfast Of Champions

This is my go to breakfast that is quick and nutritious!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Toast
  • Half an Avocado
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Fry Two Eggs
  2. Prepare Toast
  3. Spread Butter
  4. Spread Avocado
  5. Add Eggs
  6. Include salt and pepper
